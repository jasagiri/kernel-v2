## Infinite Learning

### Objective

Continue your journey with Zodiac and learn about cross chain infrastructure. **This is an optional session hosted only in the [Gnosis Guild Discord](https://discord.gg/wwmBWTgyEq) for anyone to join.**

### Preparation

1. [Zodiac Bridge tutorial](https://gnosis.github.io/zodiac/docs/tutorial-module-bridge/get-started) (2021)

### Application

- Zodiac: A multichain future
    - Review how Zodiac Bridge works
- Zodiac Bridge (50 minutes plus 5 minute Q&A)
    - Deploy a [Moloch DAO v2 framework](https://app.daohaus.club/) on xDai network
    - Deploy Zodiac Bridge Module on Rinkeby testnetwork connecting to your Moloch DAO v2 framework on xDai network
    - Submit a transaction from your Gnosis Safe on Rinkeby testnetwork to your Moloch DAO v2 framework on xDai network
